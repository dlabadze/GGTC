Overview

This module makes instantly see your PDF reports right in your browser window. You no longer have to keep downloading PDFs just to quickly check them. With a simple switch in your General Settings, all your Odoo reports that usually create PDFs will instead open directly in your browser, making it much faster to check things and review your reports.

Easily Toggle Preview Mode

Locate the new "Preview PDF Reports" section under General Settings. Simply check the "PDF Report Preview" box to enable the quick preview mode for all your Odoo reports. Uncheck it anytime to revert to the standard PDF download behavior.
